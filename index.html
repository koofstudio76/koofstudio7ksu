BIOGRAPHY

Born in the Russia, Ksenia studied at Schnittke State Institute of Music in Moscow then at the Galina Vishnevskaya Opera Centre for two years before returning to the Schnittke Institute for a post graduate degree where she has also worked as a voice teacher.
